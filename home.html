Welcome To UNICARE Hospital

Our mission is to provide quality health care to varied sections of the society at competitive cost.

Over the past 15 years, our growth has been centered on you and excellence in healthcare. Today, with a top-notch professional team, state-of-the-art equipment and modern facilities for 300 beds, UNICARE Hospital continues to offer multi super specialities right in the heart of Coimbatore city. For us, your health comes first. You are our core specialization.

Comprehensive Health Check-Ups

Regular health check-ups help detect diseases at an early stage and keep you on top of your health. At UNICARE Hospital, we offer a wide range of preventive health check packages designed for all age groups.

  • ✔️ Executive Health Check-up
  • ✔️ Cardiac Risk Assessment
  • ✔️ Diabetes & Thyroid Screening
  • ✔️ Senior Citizen Health Plans

In-House Pharmacy

At UNICARE Hospital, our fully-equipped in-house pharmacy ensures that patients receive prescribed medications quickly and safely. Staffed by certified pharmacists, we maintain a wide inventory of essential drugs and offer extended service hours to support uninterrupted, quality healthcare. We are committed to delivering professional, reliable, and patient-centered pharmaceutical services.
